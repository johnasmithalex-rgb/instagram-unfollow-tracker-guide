# Instagram Unfollow Tracker (Simple Way to Check Who Unfollowed You)

## Why I Needed This

I used to check my Instagram followers manually… scrolling through lists, trying to remember usernames, opening profiles one by one. It works for a few minutes, then it just gets frustrating.

At some point I realized there should be an easier way to figure out who unfollowed me without wasting so much time.

## What I’m Using Now

So I started using this **[Instagram unfollow tracker](https://igunfollowtracker.com/)**.

It’s pretty simple. You enter your username and it shows what changed. No login, no app, nothing complicated.

A few things I personally found useful:

- You can quickly see who unfollowed you without guessing  
- It saves a lot of time compared to manual checking  
- Helps you notice patterns in your engagement  

## Final Thoughts

If you’re trying to grow on Instagram, this actually helps more than expected. You start seeing when people drop off and what kind of content might be causing it.

I don’t use it daily or anything — just checking once a week is enough to keep things in control.

Nothing fancy here. Just a simple way to make an annoying task easier.
